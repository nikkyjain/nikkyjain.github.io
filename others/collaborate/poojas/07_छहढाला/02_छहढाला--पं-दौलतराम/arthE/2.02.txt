With a vision towards the true nature of the soul, when one truly believes in the nature of seven realities of living, non-living, inflow, bondage, stoppage, shedding and the liberation, then one obtains true faith. Therefore it is essential to know these seven realities. To understand these seven realities in the wrong way is known as the inborn wrong belief (agruhit mithyaatva). The true faith of the (seven) realties like living, non-living etc. is relevant to our purpose. In fact the characteristics of the soul is attentive consciousness (upayog). Soul is unique and sentient and not perceived with sense organs.
