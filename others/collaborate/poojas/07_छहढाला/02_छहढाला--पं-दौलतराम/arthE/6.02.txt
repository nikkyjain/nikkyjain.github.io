They (monks) meditate on the nature of their souls by controlling the activities of (their) mind, speech and body in a proper manner. Seeing their stable posture and taking them to be a slab of stone, the group of deer rub against them for (removing) their itching sensation. They do not feel attachment or aversion in liking and disliking taste, colour, smell, touch and sound to attain the status of the conquerer of the five senses.
