That kind of knowledge, which is called Sruta i.e, heard or studied, deals with things that can be stated or mentioned by means of words. While the provinces of Avadhi and Manahparyaya kinds of knowledge are things altogether distinct and different in each kind. All these things are limited and may yield to some sort of classification or division.
But in the case of knowledge, and perception of a Kevalin unlike the four kinds of knowledge no division is possible. For that knowledge and perception are absolute, perfect, endless and imperishable.
