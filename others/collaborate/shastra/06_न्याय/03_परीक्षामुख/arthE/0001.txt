The valid-knowledge (pramāna) ascertains the true nature of objects while the fallacious-knowledge (pramānābhāsa) does the opposite. Therefore, through this treatise, I (Āchārya Mānikyanandi) shall characterize, as per the earlier authoritative expositions and in brief, both these (pramāna and pramānābhāsa) for the benefit of the uninitiated learners.
