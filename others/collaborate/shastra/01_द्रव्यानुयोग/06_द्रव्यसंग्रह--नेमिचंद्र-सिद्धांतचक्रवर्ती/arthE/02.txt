Jiva is characterized by consciousness (chetana) that is concomitant with upayoga – perception (darśana) and knowledge (jnāna), is incorporeal (amūrta), a causal agent (kartā), coextensive with the body, enjoyer of the fruits of karmas (bhoktā), having the world as its abode, emancipated (Siddha), and of the nature of darting upwards.
