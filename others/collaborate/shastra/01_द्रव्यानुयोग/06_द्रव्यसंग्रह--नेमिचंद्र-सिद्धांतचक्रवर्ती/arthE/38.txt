Jivas entertaining auspicious dispositions are virtuous (punya rūpa), and those entertaining inauspicious dispositions are wicked (pāpa rūpa). Pleasant feelings (sātā vedanīya), auspicious life (shubha āyuh), auspicious name (shubha nāma), and auspicious status (shubha gotra) result from the virtuous varieties of karmas, and the remaining from the wicked varieties of karmas.
