The five-sensed jivas are categorized as those with mind (sanjnī jīvas), and those without mind (asanjnī jīvas). Rest all jīvas are without mind. The one-sensed jīvas are categorized as gross (bādara), and subtle (sūkshma). All jīvas are further categorized as having attained completion (paryāpta), and not having attained completion (aparyāpta).
