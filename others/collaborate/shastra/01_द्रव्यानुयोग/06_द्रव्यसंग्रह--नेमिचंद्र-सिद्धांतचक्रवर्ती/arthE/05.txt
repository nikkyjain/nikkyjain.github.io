Knowledge (jnana) is of eight kinds (1) sensory knowledge (matijnāna), (2) scriptural knowledge (śrutajnāna), (3) clairvoyance (avadhijnāna), (4 - 6) these three, sensory knowledge, scriptural knowledge, and clairvoyance, may also be erroneous knowledge (kumati, kuśruti, and vibhanga avadhi) when these coexist in the soul with wrong belief, (7) telepathy (manaÍparyaya jnāna), and (8) omniscience (kevalajnāna).Knowledge consciousness can also be divided into direct (pratyaksa) and indirect (paroksa) knowledge.
