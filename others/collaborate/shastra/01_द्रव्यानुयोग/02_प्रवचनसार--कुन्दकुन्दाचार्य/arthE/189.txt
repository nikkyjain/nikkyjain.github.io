Due to their quality of touch (sparsha) - greasiness or roughness - the karmic molecules - kārmāna-varganā - form new bonds between themselves. Due to its dispositions of attachment (rāga) etc. the soul (jīva) forms psychic bonds - bhāvabandha. Due to the instrumentality of each other, the soul (jīva) and the karmic molecules - kārmāna-varganā - existing in the same space form bonds - jīva-pudgala-bandha or dravyabandha.
