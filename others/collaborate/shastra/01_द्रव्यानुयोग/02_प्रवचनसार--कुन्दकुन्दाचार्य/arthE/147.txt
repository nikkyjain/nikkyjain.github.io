The substance of space (ākāsha dravya) pervades the whole of the universe (loka) and the non-universe (aloka). The substances of medium of motion (dharma dravya) and the medium of rest (adharma dravya) pervade the universe-space (lokākāsha). Denoted by transformations in the soul (jīva) and the matter (pudgala), the substance of time (kāla dravya), together with the substances of the soul (jīva dravya) and the matter (pudgala dravya), are in the universe-space (lokākāsha).
