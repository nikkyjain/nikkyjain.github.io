The substances of the soul (jīva dravya), the physical-matter (pudgalakāya), the medium of motion (dharma dravya), the medium of rest (adharma dravya) and the space (ākāsh dravya), each, has innumerable or infinite (asankhyāta or ananta) spacepoints (pradesha). The substance of time (kāla dravya) does not have multiple space-points (pradesha).
