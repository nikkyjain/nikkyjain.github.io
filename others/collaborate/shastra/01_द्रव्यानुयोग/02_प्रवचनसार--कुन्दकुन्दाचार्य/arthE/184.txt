O bhavya soul! Know that the (pure) soul (jīva) does not have the qualities of taste (rasa), colour (varna), smell (gandha), touch (sparsha), and sound (shabda), which is the mode (paryāya) of the matter (pudgala). It cannot be comprehended through any mark typical of the matter (pudgala) - alingagrahana. Its shape cannot be defined, and it has this quality of consciousness (chetanā).
