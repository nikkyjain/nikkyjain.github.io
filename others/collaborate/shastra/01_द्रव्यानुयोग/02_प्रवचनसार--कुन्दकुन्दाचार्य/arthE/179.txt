The molecules (skandha - combination of atoms), starting from those occupying two space-points (pradesha) to infinity, are produced due to their own nature of transformation. And these fine (sūkshma) and gross (sthūla) molecules of matter in form of the earth (prithivī), the water (jala), the fire (agni) and the air (vāyu) have various shapes (sansthāna, ākāra).
