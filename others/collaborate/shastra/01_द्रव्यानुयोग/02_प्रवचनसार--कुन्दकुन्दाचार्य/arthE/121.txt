This way, the substance (dravya), established in its nature, is ever endowed with origination of either the intrinsic-nature (sadbhāva-nibaddha) or the extraneous-nature (asadbhāva-nibaddha), depending on whether it is viewed from the standpoint-of-substance (dravyārthika naya) or from the standpoint-of-mode (paryāyārthika naya).
