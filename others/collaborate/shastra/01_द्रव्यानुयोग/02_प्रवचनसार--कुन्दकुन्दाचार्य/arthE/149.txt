And, the substance of time (kāla dravya) is without space-points (pradesha); it occupies just one space-point (pradesha). As the indivisible atom of matter (pudgala-paramānau) traverses slowly in the substance of space (ākāsha dravya) from one space-point to the other, the time-atom (kālānau) evolves into its mode (paryāya) of time (duration or samaya).
