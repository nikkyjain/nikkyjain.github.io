When one is convinced about his relationship with others and self, which is called sambandha, he then acts accordingly. That is called abhidheya. The next step is prayojana-siddhi, or fulfillment of the ultimate goal of one's life. - <ol><li>सम्बन्ध / relationaship - information about the living entity's eternal relationship with remaining<li>अभिधेय - to act according to that relationship<li>प्रयोजन / Ultimate Goal - Returning home, is the ultimate goal of life</ol>
