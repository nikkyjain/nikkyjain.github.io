Jiva (soul), Ajiva (non-soul), Punya (virtue), Papa (vice or sin), Asrava (influx of Karma), Samvara (stoppage of influx of Karma), Nirjara (partial shedding of previously bonded Karma), Bandha (bondage of Karma), and Moksa (liberation or complete shedding of all Karma)- comprehended from the real point of view amount to SamyagDarsana.
