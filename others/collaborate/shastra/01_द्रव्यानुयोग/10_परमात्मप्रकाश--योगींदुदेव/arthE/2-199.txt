The Jina Deva who possesses the four-fold infinite attributes, that is Kewala Darshana (perfect perception), Kewala Jnana (pure, infinite knowledge) Ananta Sukha (infinite happiness) and Ananta Virya (infinite power), is the Parama Muni (the Supreme Saint) and the same is also Parmatma Prakasha, that is the revealer of the glory of God (in His own Self).
