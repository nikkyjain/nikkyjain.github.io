Know that to be the Kala Dravya of which the Lakshana (distinguishing attribute) is Vartna (to bring about or carry on change), that is, it is a secondary cause to the Pranaman (changes) of all the objects; Anus (particles) of Kala are existing separately; as in a heap of jewels all the jewels remain separate, so is the case with Kala-Anus.
