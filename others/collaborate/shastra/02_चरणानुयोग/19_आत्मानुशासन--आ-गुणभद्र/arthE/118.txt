Even Lord ãÈabha Deva, after renouncing, like worthless blades of grass, the splendour of his kingdom and accepting austerities (tapa), had to visit without selfesteem, others’ houses for food, like a famished, poor man. He did not get it (in the proper manner) and had to roam like this for a long time (six months)*. Then, should the other men – ordinary as well as illustrious – not endure afflictions for the sake of attaining their goals?
