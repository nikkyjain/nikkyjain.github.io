How then is there the bondage of good Karmas like celestial age, etc., to saints following Ratna Traya, (a fact) well known to all persons, possible. (The answer is). Ratna Traya is the cause of Nirvana only, and of nothing else. The good Karmas which inflow, are due to the Aparadha, Defect of Shubhopayoga, good thought activity.
